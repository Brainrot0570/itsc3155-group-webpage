Our Mission Statement

We are Group 8, a team of computer science students committed to building useful, reliable, and inclusive software. Our strength is how we combine front-end polish, back-end logic, and user-centered design to deliver projects that are both functional and memorable.

Team Rules

  • Respect every voice and give constructive feedback, not personal criticism.
  • Push code in small, clear commits and write meaningful commit messages.
  • Review each other’s work before merging to main.
  • Communicate blockers early so the team can help fast.
  • Keep quality high with testing, responsive layouts, and accessible design.
